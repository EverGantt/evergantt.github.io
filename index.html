EverGantt for Mac

Gantt charts used to mean bloated enterprise software, a 14-day trial, and a sales call. EverGantt is the opposite: a small, fast, beautiful Mac app that helps you lay out a project on a timeline in minutes—then gets out of your way. Built for the person who just wants to see the plan on one screen.

Stories and Tasks on a Dragable Timeline

Drag tasks and stories across the timeline to visualize your project flow. Dependencies automatically adjust—move one task, and everything downstream shifts with it. Clean, intuitive, and precise.

Clear Months-and-Weeks View

A timeline that scales effortlessly from months to weeks without overwhelming your eyes. Adjustable row heights, resizable columns, and tunable font sizes give you density or breathing room as needed.

Smart Colors and Customization

Color-coded bars with an auto-cycling palette. Switch to fully custom colors anytime. Track team members' maximum hours per week—new in version 1.3.5—for realistic planning.

Export Anywhere, Own Your Data

Export to PNG, SVG, PDF, or CSV. Perfect for Slack screenshots, client decks, status emails, or data handoffs. Projects save as plain .evgnt files on your disk—no cloud, no lock-in.

No Distractions, Just Results

Engineers sketching quarters. Freelancers showing clients real timelines. Students mapping theses. Anyone tired of "just put it in a Gantt chart." EverGantt delivers without the bloat.

How It Works

Step 1: Create Your Project

Open EverGantt, drop in your stories and tasks. Assign durations and dependencies in seconds.

Step 2: Build the Timeline

Drag items onto the timeline. Watch dependencies auto-adjust for a realistic flow.

Step 3: Customize and Refine

Tweak colors, team hours, row heights. Zoom from months to weeks for the perfect view.

Step 4: Export and Share

Export to PNG, SVG, PDF, or CSV. Your .evgnt file stays local—email, git, or iCloud it.
